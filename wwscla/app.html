Nebraska Water Well Standards and Contractors’ Licensing Act

The Nebraska Water Well Standards and Contractors’ Licensing Act (Act) is a codified at Neb. Rev. Stat. §§ 46-1201 to 46-1241. The Act was originally enacted as LB 310 in 1986. Since then the Act has been amended in variying degrees twenty-six times. The most recent amendment was LB 148 in 2021.
